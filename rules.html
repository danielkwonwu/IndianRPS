Indian Rock Paper Scissors

Rules

  1. This game is played with 2 players, each with 30 chips.
  2. Each person is to mark 6 cards of either rock, scissor, or paper.
  3. The 12 cards are aggregated in a deck and mixed.
  4. A game of Rock, Paper, Scissors is played to determine who bets first.
  5. One card for each player is dealt face down, without the player seeing it.
  6. Immediately, the card is placed on the forehead of the players.
  7. Player who goes first places her bet.
  8. The following player can fold, call, or raise the bet.
