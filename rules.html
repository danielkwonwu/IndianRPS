Indian Rock Paper Scissors

Rules

  1. This game is played with 2 players, each with 30 chips.
  2. Each person is to mark 6 cards of either rock, scissor, or paper.
  3. The 12 cards are aggregated in a deck and mixed.
  4. A game of Rock, Paper, Scissors is played to determine who bets first.
  5. One card for each player is dealt face down, without the player seeing it.
  6. Immediately, the card is placed on the forehead of the players.
  7. Looking at the opponent's card, player who goes first places her bet.
  8. The following player can fold, call, or raise the bet.
  9. The first player then can call or fold, and this will end the betting phase.
  10. The cards are revealed simultaneously, and the winner takes the betted chips.
  11. The cards are then discarded to the discarded cards deck.
  12. 6 rounds are played if no players run out of chips.
  13. If a player runs out, she loses and the game ends.
  14. If all rounds are played, the player with more chip at the end wins.
